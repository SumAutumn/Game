This game is made of three puzzles in total. They are blackjack, sudoku, word puzzle respectively.
There are 5 chances in total. Every time, you lose one puzzle, you will lose one attempt and see the attempt left from the top right corner.
Once, the attempt becomes 0, it means this is your last chance. You will have to restart the game to play each puzzle again.
